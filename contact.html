Frequently
Asked Questions

Where is Lakewood Reserve located?

We are in the Raystown Lake region of central Pennsylvania, surrounded by forest and a short drive from the lake, trails, and local towns.

How do I book a stay?

Use the Book or Check Availability links on our site to reserve through our secure checkout, or send a message through the contact form and we will help you choose the right cabin.

What time is check-in and check-out?

Check-in starts at 4:00 PM and check-out is by 10:00 AM unless we have arranged something different with you in advance.

How many guests can stay?

Capacity depends on the cabin. The Apex sleeps up to two guests; Anchor Twenty Eight can host larger groups. Ask us to match your party to the best fit.

Are pets allowed?

Policies vary by property. Include pet details when you reach out or book so we can confirm what is possible for your dates.

Do you offer early check-in?

Early check-in depends on the prior guest departure and housekeeping. Ask in your inquiry and we will let you know if we can accommodate you.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellation terms follow the policy shown at checkout for your reservation. If you have a special situation, reply to your confirmation email or contact us and we will review options.
