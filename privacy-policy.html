Privacy Policy

Overview

Lakewood Reserve (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy. This policy describes how we may collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website or communicate with us about a stay.

Information we collect

Depending on how you interact with us, we may receive information you provide directly (for example, when you send an email or submit a contact form), as well as technical data such as browser type, device type, and general location derived from IP address, collected through standard web technologies.

How we use information

We use the information we receive to respond to inquiries, coordinate reservations and guest services, improve our website, and comply with applicable law. If you book through a third-party checkout provider, that provider’s privacy policy also applies to payment and booking data they process on our behalf.

Cookies and analytics

Our site may use cookies or similar technologies to support basic functionality, embedded content, or analytics. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Retention

We retain information only as long as needed for the purposes described above or as required by law.

Your choices

You may contact us to ask questions about this policy or to request access, correction, or deletion of personal information we hold, where applicable law allows.

Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “last updated” date at the bottom of this page will change when we do.
