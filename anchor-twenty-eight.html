Anchor Twenty Eight,
at a glance

House rules
  • No smoking inside. If smoking is permitted outdoors, use designated areas and dispose of waste safely.
  • Quiet hours 10:00 PM–7:00 AM out of respect for neighbors and wildlife.
  • Maximum occupancy is six guests unless we have confirmed otherwise in writing.
  • No parties, events, or commercial shoots without prior approval.
  • Pets only when booked and confirmed on your reservation.
  • Children should be supervised on stairs, ladders, and elevated sleeping areas.
  • Sauna and fire features: follow posted limits, never leave young children unattended, and read the safety notes in your welcome message.
  • At checkout, follow the departure checklist in your welcome message (trash, dishes, thermostat, and doors).
Amenities
  • Kitchen with cookware, serveware, and space for group meals.
  • Wi‑Fi, heating and cooling, and on-demand hot water.
  • Fresh linens, towels, and starter toiletries.
  • Private sauna and outdoor living space including a fire pit area (see guest guide for firewood and use).
  • Quick access to the lake and trail network from the property.
The space
  • Cascade-style tiny home with steps to a loft area and sleeping zones for a small group.
  • Common areas stay bright and social; sleeping nooks feel intentional rather than improvised.
  • Designed so you can spread out during the day and tuck in comfortably at night.
The experience
  • Shared breakfasts at the table, lake time when the weather cooperates, and slow evenings around the fire or in the sauna.
  • Stairs, views, and indoor–outdoor flow for guests who like a bit of adventure in the layout.
  • Modern finishes and reliable comforts throughout—without losing the treehouse feel.
Perfect for
  • Families and friend groups who want room for up to six guests.
  • Multi-day lake trips where sauna evenings and fire pit time are part of the plan.
  • Guests who enjoy a unique floor plan and do not mind navigating steps to the loft area.
